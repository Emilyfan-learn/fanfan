高中英文學測翻譯解題指南

題目 1:青少年身心發展

1.1 隨著青少年進入了青春期,他們逐漸注意到身體上的變化。

技巧點判斷:

  • 「隨著...」表時間/條件關係 → 使用 As 引導副詞子句
  • 「逐漸」表漸進過程 → gradually/progressively
  • 「注意到」→ notice/become aware of

解題策略:

  1. 識別主要子句與從屬子句
  2. 時間副詞子句用 As 開頭
  3. 主詞為 teenagers/adolescents
  4. 動詞時態:現在簡單式(陳述普遍事實)

句型結構圖:

As + S1 + V1 (進入青春期), S2 + V2 (注意到) + O (變化)
     ↓                        ↓
[時間副詞子句]              [主要子句]

範文:

As teenagers enter puberty, they gradually notice physical changes.

關鍵句標示:

  • As teenagers enter puberty (時間副詞子句)
  • gradually notice (漸進動詞)
  • physical changes (受詞)

實用句型:

  • As + S + V, S + V (隨著...)
  • gradually + V (逐漸...)
  • notice/become aware of + N (注意到...)

1.2 他們開始在意自己的外表,並且對異性感到好奇。

技巧點判斷:

  • 「開始...」→ begin/start to + V
  • 「在意」→ care about/be concerned about
  • 「並且」表並列關係 → and 連接兩個動詞片語
  • 「對...感到好奇」→ be/become curious about

解題策略:

  1. 使用對等連接詞 and 連接兩個平行結構
  2. 兩個動詞片語:care about / become curious about
  3. 注意平行結構的一致性

句型結構圖:

S + begin to + V1 (care about) + O1 + and + V2 (become curious about) + O2
              ↓                            ↓
         [第一個動作]                  [第二個動作]

範文:

They begin to care about their appearance and become curious about the opposite sex.

關鍵句標示:

  • begin to care about their appearance (第一個動作)
  • and (對等連接詞)
  • become curious about the opposite sex (第二個動作)

實用句型:

  • begin/start to + V (開始...)
  • care about/be concerned about + N (在意...)
  • become curious about + N (對...感到好奇)
  • A and B (並列結構)

題目 2:約會情境

2.1 在一些練習後,Jack 終於鼓起勇氣約 Jane 共進晚餐。

技巧點判斷:

  • 「在...之後」→ After + N/Ving
  • 「終於」表時間遞進 → finally/eventually
  • 「鼓起勇氣」慣用語 → gather/pluck up the courage to
  • 「約...做某事」→ ask/invite sb to + V

解題策略:

  1. 使用介系詞片語 After... 作時間副詞
  2. 注意慣用語的正確搭配
  3. 不定詞表目的

句型結構圖:

After + N/Ving, S + Adv (finally) + V (gathered courage) + to V (ask/invite)
     ↓                                                        ↓
[時間副詞片語]                                           [目的不定詞]

範文:

After some practice, Jack finally gathered the courage to ask Jane to have dinner with him.

或

After practicing several times, Jack finally plucked up the courage to invite Jane to dinner.

關鍵句標示:

  • After some practice (時間副詞片語)
  • finally (副詞)
  • gathered the courage to ask (慣用語 + 不定詞)

實用句型:

  • After + N/Ving (在...之後)
  • finally/eventually (終於)
  • gather/pluck up the courage to + V (鼓起勇氣做...)
  • ask/invite sb to + V (邀請某人做...)

2.2 令他很失望的是,Jane 婉拒了他的邀約因為她已事前有約。

技巧點判斷:

  • 「令人...的是」→ To one's + 情緒名詞 (To his disappointment)
  • 「婉拒」→ politely decline/turn down
  • 「因為」表原因 → because/since/as
  • 「事前有約」→ have a prior commitment/engagement

解題策略:

  1. 使用 To his disappointment 開頭表達情緒
  2. 主要動詞:declined/turned down
  3. 原因子句說明理由

句型結構圖:

To one's + 情緒N, S + V (declined) + O + because + S + V (已有約)
     ↓                                        ↓
[表情緒的副詞片語]                        [原因副詞子句]

範文:

To his disappointment, Jane politely declined/turned down his invitation because she already had a prior commitment.

關鍵句標示:

  • To his disappointment (情緒副詞片語)
  • politely declined/turned down (婉拒的表達)
  • because she already had a prior commitment (原因子句)

實用句型:

  • To one's disappointment/surprise/delight (令某人...)
  • politely decline/turn down (婉拒)
  • because/since/as (因為)
  • have a prior commitment/engagement (已有約)

題目 3:熱戀情侶

3.1 在交往一段時間後,Joan 和 Mike 陷入熱戀。

技巧點判斷:

  • 「在...之後」→ After + Ving/N
  • 「交往」→ date/go out (together)
  • 「一段時間」→ for a while/for some time
  • 「陷入熱戀」慣用語 → fall deeply in love/be madly in love

解題策略:

  1. 時間副詞片語置於句首
  2. 使用過去式(描述過去發生的事)
  3. 注意慣用語搭配

句型結構圖:

After + Ving (dating) + for a while, S + V (fell in love)
     ↓                                   ↓
[時間副詞片語]                      [主要子句]

範文:

After dating for a while, Joan and Mike fell deeply in love.

或

After going out together for some time, Joan and Mike became madly in love.

關鍵句標示:

  • After dating for a while (時間副詞片語)
  • fell deeply in love (慣用語)

實用句型:

  • After + Ving + for a while/for some time (在...一段時間後)
  • fall (deeply) in love (陷入熱戀)
  • be madly in love (熱戀中)

3.2 他們會講好幾個小時的電話,而且沒有任何事物能阻止他們在一起。

技巧點判斷:

  • 「會...」表習慣 → would + V/used to + V
  • 「好幾個小時」→ for hours
  • 「而且」表遞進 → and (連接兩個子句)
  • 「沒有任何事物」→ nothing (否定代名詞)
  • 「阻止...做某事」→ stop/prevent/keep sb from Ving

解題策略:

  1. 第一個子句:描述習慣性動作
  2. 第二個子句:使用否定句強調
  3. 注意 prevent/stop/keep 的用法差異

句型結構圖:

S + would + V (talk) + for hours, and + nothing + could + V (stop) + sb + from + Ving
           ↓                                                              ↓
    [習慣性動作]                                                    [阻止某人做某事]

範文:

They would talk on the phone for hours, and nothing could stop/prevent them from being together.

關鍵句標示:

  • would talk on the phone for hours (習慣性動作)
  • nothing could stop them from being together (否定句 + 慣用語)

實用句型:

  • would + V (過去習慣...)
  • talk on the phone for hours (講電話好幾小時)
  • nothing can/could + V (沒有任何事物能...)
  • stop/prevent/keep sb from Ving (阻止某人做...)

題目 4:情侶關係研究

4.1 研究指出年輕的情侶容易因為很微不足道的事情分手。

技巧點判斷:

  • 「研究指出」→ Research shows/indicates that...
  • 「容易...」→ tend to + V/be likely to + V
  • 「分手」→ break up
  • 「因為」→ over/because of (介系詞)
  • 「微不足道的」→ trivial/minor/insignificant

解題策略:

  1. 主詞為 Research,後接 that 名詞子句
  2. 名詞子句中描述研究結果
  3. 注意介系詞 over 的特殊用法(表原因)

句型結構圖:

Research + shows/indicates + that + S + tend to + V (break up) + over + Adj + N
                              ↓                                      ↓
                        [名詞子句]                            [原因介系詞片語]

範文:

Research shows/indicates that young couples tend to break up over trivial/minor matters.

或

Studies suggest that young couples are likely to break up because of insignificant things.

關鍵句標示:

  • Research shows that (研究指出)
  • tend to break up (容易分手)
  • over trivial matters (因為瑣事)

實用句型:

  • Research shows/indicates that... (研究指出...)
  • tend to + V/be likely to + V (容易/傾向於...)
  • break up (分手)
  • over/because of + N (因為...)
  • trivial/minor/insignificant (微不足道的)

4.2 如果他們學會傾聽對方並一同解決問題,衝突很可能幫助改善他們的關係。

技巧點判斷:

  • 「如果」表條件 → If 引導條件子句
  • 「學會做某事」→ learn to + V
  • 「傾聽對方」→ listen to each other
  • 「並」表並列 → and
  • 「一同」→ together/jointly
  • 「很可能」→ be likely to/can possibly
  • 「改善」→ improve/enhance

解題策略:

  1. If 條件句 + 主要子句結構
  2. 條件句中使用 learn to 表達「學會」
  3. 主要子句表達可能的結果

句型結構圖:

If + S + learn to + V1 (listen) + and + V2 (solve), S + be likely to + V (improve)
     ↓                                                   ↓
[條件副詞子句]                                      [主要子句-結果]

範文:

If they learn to listen to each other and solve problems together, conflicts are likely to help improve their relationship.

或

If they learn to listen to one another and work together to solve problems, conflicts can possibly help enhance their relationship.

關鍵句標示:

  • If they learn to listen to each other (條件子句)
  • and solve problems together (並列動作)
  • are likely to help improve (可能性 + 改善)

實用句型:

  • If + S + V, S + V (如果...就...)
  • learn to + V (學會...)
  • listen to each other/one another (傾聽對方)
  • solve problems together (一同解決問題)
  • be likely to + V (很可能...)
  • help + V/help to + V (幫助...)
  • improve/enhance (改善)

題目 5:打工經驗

5.1 除了賺些零用錢外,打工幫助學生獲得實用的技能。

技巧點判斷:

  • 「除了...外」表附加 → Besides/In addition to + N/Ving
  • 「賺零用錢」→ earn pocket money/make some extra money
  • 「打工」→ part-time jobs/working part-time
  • 「幫助某人做某事」→ help sb (to) + V
  • 「獲得」→ acquire/gain/develop
  • 「實用的」→ practical/useful

解題策略:

  1. 介系詞片語置於句首表附加資訊
  2. 主詞為 part-time jobs
  3. 使用 help 的正確句型

句型結構圖:

Besides/In addition to + Ving (earning), S (part-time jobs) + help + sb + (to) V (acquire)
            ↓                                                              ↓
    [介系詞片語-附加]                                              [不定詞-目的]

範文:

Besides/In addition to earning pocket money, part-time jobs help students acquire/gain practical skills.

或

Besides making some extra money, working part-time helps students develop useful skills.

關鍵句標示:

  • Besides earning pocket money (附加資訊)
  • help students acquire (幫助獲得)
  • practical skills (實用技能)

實用句型:

  • Besides/In addition to + N/Ving (除了...外)
  • earn pocket money/make extra money (賺零用錢)
  • part-time job (打工)
  • help sb (to) + V (幫助某人...)
  • acquire/gain/develop (獲得/培養)
  • practical/useful (實用的)

5.2 從這些工作經驗,他們也能夠探索自己的潛能,甚至找到人生的目標。

技巧點判斷:

  • 「從...」表來源 → From/Through + N
  • 「能夠」→ be able to/can
  • 「探索」→ explore/discover
  • 「潛能」→ potential/capabilities
  • 「甚至」表遞進 → even/and even
  • 「人生目標」→ life goal/purpose in life

解題策略:

  1. 介系詞片語 From... 置於句首
  2. 使用 and even 連接兩個遞進的動作
  3. 注意平行結構

句型結構圖:

From/Through + N (experiences), S + can + V1 (explore) + and even + V2 (find)
         ↓                                                            ↓
  [介系詞片語-來源]                                              [遞進並列]

範文:

From these work experiences, they can also explore their potential and even find their purpose in life.

或

Through such job experiences, they are able to discover their capabilities and even find goals in life.

關鍵句標示:

  • From these work experiences (來源)
  • can explore their potential (能夠探索)
  • and even find their purpose in life (甚至找到目標)

實用句型:

  • From/Through + N (從...)
  • be able to/can (能夠)
  • explore/discover (探索)
  • potential/capabilities (潛能)
  • and even (甚至)
  • purpose/goal in life (人生目標)

題目 6:親子教養

6.1 父母親應該要鼓勵小孩獨立,而非過度保護或溺愛。

技巧點判斷:

  • 「應該」→ should
  • 「鼓勵某人做某事」→ encourage sb to + V
  • 「獨立」→ be independent/become independent
  • 「而非」表對比 → rather than/instead of
  • 「過度保護」→ overprotect
  • 「溺愛」→ spoil/pamper
  • 「或」→ or

解題策略:

  1. 使用 should 表建議
  2. encourage 後接不定詞
  3. rather than 連接對比概念

句型結構圖:

S (Parents) + should + V (encourage) + sb + to V, rather than + V1 or V2
                                              ↓            ↓
                                         [正面建議]   [負面對比]

範文:

Parents should encourage their children to be independent rather than overprotect or spoil them.

或

Parents should encourage their children to become independent instead of overprotecting or pampering them.

關鍵句標示:

  • should encourage their children to be independent (應該鼓勵)
  • rather than overprotect or spoil them (對比-不該做的)

實用句型:

  • should + V (應該)
  • encourage sb to + V (鼓勵某人...)
  • be/become independent (獨立)
  • rather than/instead of (而非)
  • overprotect (過度保護)
  • spoil/pamper (溺愛)

6.2 事實上,小孩應該為自己的行為承擔後果,而非凡事依賴父母。

技巧點判斷:

  • 「事實上」→ In fact/Actually
  • 「為...承擔後果」→ take responsibility for/bear the consequences of
  • 「行為」→ behavior/actions
  • 「而非」→ rather than/instead of
  • 「凡事」→ everything/in everything
  • 「依賴」→ depend on/rely on

解題策略:

  1. 使用轉折語 In fact 開頭
  2. 對比兩種行為:承擔責任 vs 依賴
  3. 注意 rather than 後的動詞形式

句型結構圖:

In fact, S + should + V (take responsibility), rather than + V (depend on)
             ↓                                              ↓
      [應該的行為]                                    [不該的行為]

範文:

In fact, children should take responsibility for their own behavior/actions rather than depend/rely on their parents for everything.

或

Actually, children should bear the consequences of their actions instead of relying on their parents in everything.

關鍵句標示:

  • In fact (轉折語)
  • should take responsibility for their actions (承擔責任)
  • rather than depend on their parents for everything (對比)

實用句型:

  • In fact/Actually (事實上)
  • take responsibility for (為...負責)
  • bear the consequences of (承擔...的後果)
  • one's own behavior/actions (自己的行為)
  • rather than/instead of (而非)
  • depend on/rely on (依賴)
  • for everything/in everything (凡事)

題目 7:大學畢業生

7.1 許多學生即將要從大學畢業時,會對於未來感到困惑。

技巧點判斷:

  • 「當...時」→ When/As 引導時間子句
  • 「即將要」表未來 → be about to + V/be going to + V
  • 「從...畢業」→ graduate from
  • 「會...」表傾向 → tend to/are likely to
  • 「對...感到困惑」→ feel confused about/be confused about

解題策略:

  1. When 時間子句 + 主要子句結構
  2. 時間子句使用未來式或現在式
  3. 主要子句描述結果

句型結構圖:

When/As + S + be about to + V (graduate), S + V (feel confused) + about + N
          ↓                                   ↓
   [時間副詞子句]                        [主要子句]

範文:

When many students are about to graduate from college/university, they tend to feel confused about their future.

或

As many students are going to graduate from university, they are likely to be confused about the future.

關鍵句標示:

  • When students are about to graduate (時間子句)
  • tend to feel confused about (感到困惑)
  • their future (未來)

實用句型:

  • When/As + S + V (當...時)
  • be about to + V/be going to + V (即將...)
  • graduate from (從...畢業)
  • tend to/be likely to (傾向於/會...)
  • feel confused about/be confused about (對...感到困惑)

7.2 此時,就是正面的態度將給予他們力量去面對人生中新的挑戰。

技巧點判斷:

  • 「此時」→ At this moment/At this time/It is then
  • 「就是...」強調句 → It is... that...
  • 「正面的態度」→ positive attitude
  • 「將」表未來 → will
  • 「給予某人某物」→ give sb sth
  • 「力量去做...」→ strength to + V
  • 「面對」→ face/confront

解題策略:

  1. 使用強調句型 It is... that... 強調「正面態度」
  2. 或使用 At this time 作時間副詞
  3. 不定詞 to face 表目的

句型結構圖:

It is + N (positive attitude) + that + will + V (give) + sb + N (strength) + to V (face)
   ↓                                                                          ↓
[強調句型]                                                              [目的不定詞]

範文:

At this moment/time, it is a positive attitude that will give them the strength to face new challenges in life.

或

It is then that a positive attitude will give them strength to confront the new challenges of life.

或 (非強調句型):

At this time, a positive attitude will give them the strength to face new challenges in life.

關鍵句標示:

  • It is a positive attitude that (強調句型)
  • will give them the strength (給予力量)
  • to face new challenges in life (面對挑戰)

實用句型:

  • At this moment/time/It is then (此時)
  • It is... that... (強調句型)
  • positive attitude (正面態度)
  • give sb sth (給予某人某物)
  • the strength to + V (去做...的力量)
  • face/confront (面對)
  • challenges in life/life challenges (人生挑戰)

總結:翻譯技巧要點

常用連接詞整理

  1. 時間關係: As, When, After, Before
  2. 因果關係: because, since, as
  3. 對比關係: rather than, instead of, while
  4. 遞進關係: and even, not only... but also
  5. 條件關係: If

常用句型結構

  1. S + V + O (基本句型)
  2. 副詞子句 + 主要子句 (複合句)
  3. It is... that... (強調句)
  4. S + help + sb + (to) V (使役動詞)
  5. S + encourage/allow + sb + to V (不定詞補語)

翻譯注意事項

  1. 確認時態(現在/過去/未來)
  2. 識別主被動語態
  3. 注意慣用語搭配
  4. 保持句子平衡與對稱
  5. 善用連接詞表達邏輯關係
